KK Profiling: Barangay LOOC

Under the SK Reform Act of 2015 (RA 10742), the Sangguniang Kabataan is conducting Katipunan ng Kabataan (KK) Profiling for individuals aged fifteen (15) to thirty (30) in our barangay. This initiative aims to gather crucial data about our youth. KK Profiling helps us develop programs and projects that connect with the interests and needs of our youth. Your cooperation is essential for this initiative to thrive. Rest assured, all information collected will be kept confidential and used solely for profiling purposes. NOTE: ONGOING ang atung SK Officials and Looc Youth Task Force sa pag conduct ug KK Profiling per Purok Thank you for actively participating in shaping the future of Barangay Looc.
